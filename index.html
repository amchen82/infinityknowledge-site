Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Infinity Knowledges ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Cute Dragon Blaster, Ink Island, and FIRE Calculator mobile applications. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our apps.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information. Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — displays advertisements. May collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — handles all purchases and subscriptions per Apple's privacy policy.

Advertising

Our app displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, our app requests permission to track you via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before any tracking occurs. You can change this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → [App Name].

Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Game progress is stored locally on your device only.

Children's Privacy

Our app is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
